Channel Partner Terms & Conditions
  1. Lead Tagging at Site Visit

    Channel Partners must accompany the customer on their first site visit in order to be eligible for lead credit.

  2. No Prior Registration Required

    The current lead registration system will be discontinued.
    No advance lead registration is required under the new system.

  3. Credit Policy

    Credit will be awarded to the first Channel Partner who brings the customer for a site visit.

  4. Exceptions:
    • Company-generated Digital/Reference leads are not eligible for partner credit.
    • However, if such a lead is in any of the following statuses — Ringing No Response (RNR), Non-Contactable, Lost, or Not Interested — and a Channel Partner successfully brings the customer for a visit, credit will be awarded to the Channel Partner.
  5. Booking Conversion Window

    A 45-day window from the date of the first site visit will be provided for booking conversion.
    If the lead is not converted within this period, it will move to the Lost stage.
    After 45 days, the same customer can be brought in again by any Channel Partner as a new lead for a fresh site visit.

  6. No Credit for Pre-Registered Leads

    Leads that are already registered cannot be considered. Therefore, please accompany the customers during site visits, and ensure proper tagging is done.

We believe this new approach will bring greater clarity, fairness, and efficiency to the lead management process.

Should you have any questions or require further clarification, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.
